    About Rejoys

    Professional Event Organiser

    My REJOYS journey into the world of event planning and design began with a simple passion for catering and creating beautiful culinary experiences. What started as a love for crafting cheese platters and curries quickly evolved into something much bigger when friends began to ask for my help in planning and designing their weddings.

    As a friend, I volunteered to assist, and soon found myself hosting weddings and organising events for hundreds of guests. The overwhelming positive response from attendees encouraged me to explore the possibility of turning my passion into a business. With each event, I witnessed the power of bringing people together and creating unforgettable memories.

    At Rejoys, we envision crafting transformative experiences that inspire, delight, and leave a lasting impact. Guided by the empowering force of love and passion, we believe that each occasion is an opportunity to create magical moments, forge meaningful connections, and spread happiness far and wide. We provide management and coordination of events include:

    • Corporate Events:
      With our rich and successful background in corporate event planning, we are thrilled to announce our privilege in hosting one of the biggest corporate events in early March - the launch of the top 10 solar company's product at Park Hyatt Sydney Hotel. Leveraging our extensive experience, we are dedicated to delivering an unforgettable and seamless experience for our clients, featuring distinguished guests and top-class service.
